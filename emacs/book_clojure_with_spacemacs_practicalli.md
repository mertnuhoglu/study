### Evaluating Clojure

``` bash
SPC m
e				eval menu
e e			eval expression before cursor	cider-eval-last-sexp
e w			eval and replace its result	cider-eval-last-sexp-and-replace
e m			expand macro	cider-macroexpand-1
``` 

Inspecting:

``` bash
SPC m or ,
d v			cider-inspector
``` 

REPL Buffer:

``` bash

```

  | , e f   | eval top level expr                     |
